Donating Stem Cells Through Bone Marrow


Step 1: We send you and your chosen companion to a hotel overnight before you head to the hospital the next day. Our treat!

Step 2: Today's the Day! You are becoming a Hero! In the morning, you will get a general anesthetic.

Step 3: Now it's time for a short procedure; it should take about 30 minutes. It's not hard, you just lie on your side and bone marrow is removed from your pelvic bone using a needle and syringe. At the end of the procedure, you will have 2 plasters covering the places the bone marrow was taken from and thats it.

MYTH: Donating bone marrow is a really painful process.
FACT: Most donors say that its similar to how you feel after an intense workout at the gym.

Step 4: When you wake up, you may feel bruised from the needles. Your friend arrives. You will have to spend that day and night recovering from the anesthetic, before heading home the next day.
